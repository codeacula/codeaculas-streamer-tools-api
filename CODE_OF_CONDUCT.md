# Rules & Code of Conduct

## **General Rules**

1. **Respect Everyone**
   Treat all members with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bigotry of any kind will **not** be tolerated. If you attack members of our community, you will be removed. **No warnings. No debates.**

2. **No Spamming**
   Avoid excessive posting, irrelevant links, or disruptive behavior. Keep the space clean and productive.

3. **Keep It Civil & Constructive**
   Discussions and debates are welcome, but **bad-faith arguments, personal attacks, or toxicity are not**. **Human rights are NOT up for debate** here. If you're here to argue people's existence, **leave now.**

4. **Follow Discord's Terms of Service**
   Any violation of Discord's policies will result in immediate removal from the server.

5. **Use Threads**
   Outside of #common-room, use threads for long-running conversations to keep discussions organized.

6. **Support Economic & Social Equity**
   We believe in a world where people are treated fairly, including fair wages, labor rights, and reducing systemic inequality. Classism, elitism, or justifying poverty as a "personal failure" will **not** be tolerated.

---

## **Programming-Specific Rules**

1. **All Questions Are Welcome**
   No gatekeeping. Whether you're a beginner or a senior dev, you're encouraged to ask, learn, and share knowledge.

2. **Career Guidance Encouraged**
   Feel free to ask about breaking into tech, job challenges, and career growth. We’re here to support each other.

3. **Share Knowledge Thoughtfully**
   If sharing advice that could help others, consider creating a GitHub Gist or a public resource link to benefit the entire community.

---

## **Gaming-Specific Rules**

1. **Use Spoilers**
   If you're discussing plot details, use spoiler tags (`||text||`) or put it in a thread.

---

## **Miscellaneous Notes**

1. **Feel Free to Self-Promote**
   We have #town-crier for announcing streams and content. If you're a creator, we support you.
